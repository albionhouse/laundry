Steamup Iron

instructions

IMPORTANT SAFETY MEASURES

Use this appliance only for ironing.

Certain parts of the electrical device are very hot during use.

Touching the surface can cause burns.

Never iron clothes on the body.

The iron must be placed only on the iron rack.

Do not touch the iron rack during or after ironing as it becomes very hot and can cause burns.

REFILLING

If the water level indicator is lit red and the buzzer sounds, the water tank must be filled.

The water tank can be filled at any time; it is not necessary to let the boiler cool beforehand.

Caution: Always fill the water tank before you start ironing.

IRONING

When switched on, half the water tank is automatically pumped into the boiler. The pumping noise you hear is normal.

Wait around 3 minutes for the steam indicator and the iron indicator to both be lit green before you start ironing.

TEMPERATURE SETTING OF THE IRON

After changing the setting, it takes around 2 minutes until the new temperature is reached.
Set the temperature higher if the iron "spits water".

STEAM BUTTON

Before starting ironing or after taking an extended break, press the steam button 2 or 3 times to let any condensed water possibly in the steam hose to escape.

USING THE SOFT PRESSING SOLE
Using the soft pressing sole is particularly important when ironing dark fabrics, as well as synthetic fabrics that can often stick to the iron.
Attention: The sole is very hot during and after use. Do not touch. Contact can cause burns.
